History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a platform where innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs could share knowledge and shape the future of technology. Over the years, it has evolved into a global event attracting thousands of participants annually.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, promote collaboration, and inspire the next generation of technology leaders. TechCon provides a space for learning, networking, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a variety of exceptional minds who have transformed the tech landscape through innovation, research, and leadership.

Ada Obiora

Ada Obiora is a renowned data scientist and AI ethics advocate. At TechCon 2022, she spoke on "The Human Side of Artificial Intelligence", emphasising the importance of responsible and inclusive tech development.

Jonathan Ogundele

Jonathan Ogundele, CTO of GreenNet, delivered an inspiring keynote at TechCon 2023 about sustainable technology solutions. His work focuses on using green energy and IoT to reduce digital carbon footprints.
